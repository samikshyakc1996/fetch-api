BMR

Basal Metabolic Rate is the number of calories required to keep your body functioning at rest.
Your BMR can be used to help you gain, lose, or maintain your weight. By knowing how many calories you burn, you can know how many to consume.

BMI

Body mass index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight that applies to adult men and women.
A reliable way to determine whether a person has too much body fat is to calculate the ratio of their weight to their height squared. This ratio, called the body mass index (BMI).
